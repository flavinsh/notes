$$\Large{{V(X+Y)=\text{V}(X)+2\text{Cov}(X,Y)+\text{V}(Y)}}$$

> [!proof]- 
> $$\begin{flalign*}
> \text{V}(X+Y)
> &= \text{Cov}(X+Y, X+Y) \\
> &= \text{Cov}(X,X+Y)+\text{Cov}(Y,X+Y)\\
> &= \text{Cov}(X,X)+\text{Cov}(X,Y)+\text{Cov}(Y,X)+\text{Cov}(Y,Y)\\
> &= \text{V}(X)+2\text{Cov}(X,Y)+\text{V}(Y)\\
> \end{flalign*}$$
