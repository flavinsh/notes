The **population mean** $(\mu)$ is the arithmetic [[mean]] of a population:
$$\Large{\mu=\frac{\sum\limits_{i=1}^{N} X_{i}}{N}}$$
where $N$ is the number of observations in the population.

It is a measure of central tendency.
